Recycled Table

An Ikea rug that was ready to be thrown away + some wood boards I found at school + couple of wood sticks+ some rusty nails = a table that was supposed to be a chair After moving into my new apartment, I found that I need a chair to sit on when I’m changing shoes. So I decided to make one myself. My roommate was about to throw away that rug, and I remembered I saw there were some unwanted boards in the school MakerSpace. I thought I could put them together and give them a new life. However, I noticed the board was too large to be a chair, and out of proportion. So I decided to make it to be a table, which I could sit on anyway.
